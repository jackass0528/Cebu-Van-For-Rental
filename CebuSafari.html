    ║WHAT TO EXPECT

    Exclusive Privacy: This tour package offers you and your group the opportunity to explore the Cebu Safari and Adventure Park in a private and intimate setting. Enjoy the park's attractions without the crowds, ensuring a personalized and memorable experience.

    Wildlife Encounters: Immerse yourself in the diverse and exotic wildlife of Cebu Safari and Adventure Park. Encounter animals from around the world, including majestic African lions, gentle giraffes, playful zebras, and many more. Your private tour allows for up-close and unhurried interactions with these incredible creatures.


    Safari Adventure: Embark on a thrilling safari adventure through the park's expansive savannah, where you can witness free-roaming animals in a habitat that closely resembles their natural environments. Get ready for awe-inspiring photo opportunities as you observe these animals in a more authentic setting.

    Scenic Views: The Cebu Safari and Adventure Park is situated amid breathtaking natural beauty, offering lush forests, rolling hills, and panoramic vistas. Capture the beauty of the Philippine countryside while exploring the park.


    Educational Opportunities: Learn about the park's conservation efforts and the importance of wildlife preservation through informative guided tours. Gain a deeper understanding of the animals' natural behaviors, habitats, and the global efforts to protect endangered species.


    Zipline and Adventure Activities: For the adventurous at heart, enjoy adrenaline-pumping activities such as ziplining, ATV rides, and trekking. Soar above the park's stunning landscape and take in breathtaking views of the surrounding scenery.


    Tour Logistics: Duration: The tour duration is customizable based on your preferences, ranging from half-day to full-day adventures.


    Transportation: Private transportation to and from the park is provided, ensuring a comfortable and hassle-free journey.


    Guide: A knowledgeable and friendly guide will accompany you throughout the tour, providing insights, answering questions, and ensuring your safety.


    Who Should Book This Tour: Families looking for an educational and fun outing. Nature and animal lovers seeking an up-close wildlife experience.
