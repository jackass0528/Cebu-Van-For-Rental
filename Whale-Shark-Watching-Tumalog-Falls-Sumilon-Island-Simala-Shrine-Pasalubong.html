    ║WHAT TO EXPECT


    Whale Shark Watching: Your adventure begins with a visit to Oslob, a renowned destination for whale shark watching. Here, you'll have the opportunity to swim and snorkel alongside these gentle giants in the azure waters of Tan-awan. It's a mesmerizing encounter with the world's largest fish and a must-do experience for nature enthusiasts.

    Tumalog Falls: After your exhilarating time with whale sharks, the tour takes you to Tumalog Falls, located nearby in Oslob. These cascading waterfalls, often referred to as the "Cebu's Niagara Falls," offer a serene and refreshing escape. You can take a dip in the cool waters or simply enjoy the breathtaking scenery and lush surroundings.

    Simala Shrine: The tour then heads to Sibonga, where you'll find the awe-inspiring Simala Shrine, also known as the Monastery of the Holy Eucharist. This magnificent architectural marvel is a place of worship and pilgrimage for devotees of the Blessed Virgin Mary. The shrine's intricate design, ornate details, and tranquil atmosphere make it a captivating stop for those interested in religious and cultural experiences.

    Cebu Pasalubong: To wrap up the day, the tour includes a visit to local souvenir shops in Cebu. Here, you can shop for unique Cebuano crafts, delicacies, and souvenirs to bring back home. From dried mangoes to handcrafted items, you'll have the chance to purchase mementos of your Cebu adventure..
