    ║WHAT TO EXPECT

    Get up close and personal with the gentle giants of the sea as you embark on an awe-inspiring adventure to witness whale sharks in their natural habitat. Swim alongside these magnificent creatures for an unforgettable experience.

    Discover the pristine beauty of Sumilon Island, known for its crystal-clear waters, white sandy beaches, and vibrant marine life. Snorkel, swim, or simply relax on the beach as you soak in the island's serenity.

    Immerse yourself in the lush tropical paradise of Kawasan Falls. Marvel at the cascading waterfalls and turquoise pools, perfect for a refreshing dip. You can also opt for adventurous activities like canyoneering and trekking in this natural wonder.
