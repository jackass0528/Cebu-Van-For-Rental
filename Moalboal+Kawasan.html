Moalboal + Kawasan falls

The Cheapest Van Rental Agency In Cebu City, Philippines

  • Duration: 12 Hours
  • 48-Hour Cancellation
  • Available Daily

Moalboal is a small town of Cebu located in the southern part of Cebu island. It is one of the best known beaches you could go since it has colorful underwater with many beautiful corals and different species fishes (Sardines).

After your Moalboal snorkeling activity you can visit the famous and beautiful Kawasan Waterfalls the is know to be the top destination in Cebu Island.


NOTE: Below 7 years old are not allowed in kawasan falls
