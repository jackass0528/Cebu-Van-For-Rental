    ⊳MAPS

    ║WHAT TO EXPECT

    Cebu City: Start your journey in the vibrant Cebu City, where you can explore historical sites like Magellan's Cross, Fort San Pedro, and the Basilica del Santo Niño. Don't forget to try the local cuisine at bustling markets and restaurants.

    Simala Shrine: A pilgrimage site famous for its stunning architecture and religious significance. Marvel at the intricate details and spend some time in prayer or reflection.

    Whale Shark Watching: Head to Oslob for an unforgettable encounter with gentle giants of the sea. Snorkel alongside whale sharks, the world's largest fish, in their natural habitat.

    Sumilon Island: A short boat ride away from Oslob, Sumilon Island offers pristine beaches, crystal-clear waters, and great opportunities for swimming, snorkeling, or simply relaxing on the white sand.

    Kawasan Falls: Journey to the lush greenery of Badian to experience the captivating beauty of Kawasan Falls. Hike through tropical forest trails to reach these multi-tiered waterfalls, where you can cool off and even try canyoneering for an adrenaline rush.

    Moalboal Sardines Run: Cap off your adventure in Moalboal, where you can witness an awe-inspiring natural phenomenon—the Sardines Run. Thousands of sardines move in synchronized patterns, creating a mesmerizing underwater spectacle perfect for snorkeling or diving.
